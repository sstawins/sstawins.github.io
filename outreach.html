Physcis & Astronomy Community Excellence

PACE is a graduate student led peer-mentoring program at UCI. The goal of the program is to help graduate students at all levels to be successful in grad school as well as teach our mentors valuable leadership and mentorship skills. In my second year at UCI, I joined the PACE leadership team and now am running the program with three other team members. Our programs include - Exceleration (1-on-1 grad peer mentoring for first-year graduates), Fire Up Fridays (bi-weekly group workshops for incoming grads), and Attitude Thrusters (group workshops for graduates at all levels). PACE is very thankful to be funded by AAS - National Osterbrock Leadership Program (NOLP).
