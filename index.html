About Me...

I am a third year Astrophysics Ph.D student at University of California, Irvine studying galaxy evolution. In 2019, I earned my MS in Astronomy at San Diego State University studying gas around galaxies and its effects on the central galaxy. My current projects center around finding Lya emission from the most distant glaaxies.
Apart from research, I enjoy participating in astronomy/science outreach.
